## Preparation

### DAIDAI ★

DAIDAI is an NPC that offers a daily quest which gives EXP buff by 100% for 1 hour.
The quest can be accepted since level 1.
<Collapse title="DAIDAI location">
<span>![DAIDAI](https://ro.ginyuki.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/daidai.png)
Image by [Ginyuki](https://ginyuki.com)</span>
</Collapse>

### Town Mules

Town Mules are easy to make and always useful.

<Collapse title="Merchant">
<span>Very good first character to increase Zeny efficiency due to [Overcharge](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Overcharge), [Discount](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Discount) and optionally [Vending](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Vending).</span>
</Collapse>

<Collapse title="Acolyte (Warp Portal)">
<span>Save travel time by using [Warp Portal](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Warp_Portal)</span>
</Collapse>

### Battle Mules

Battle Mules are support classes that will help your main character by boosting damage, movement speed, hp, sp and etc. These takes a while to make as they need to be high level.

<Collapse title="Cardinal">
<span>Obviously the best support class as it's meant to be a support class. Example of QOL skills are [Cantocandidus](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Cantocandidus), [Clementia](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Clementia),
[Convenio](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Convenio) and [Renovatio](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Renovatio)</span>
</Collapse>

<Collapse title="Troubadour/Trouvere">
<span>4th job is required since they can solely buff due to [Kvasir Sonata](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Kvasir_Sonata), which will let them use
[Mental Sensing](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Mental_Sensing) (EXP Buff) and
[Song of Mana](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Song_of_Mana) (Unlimited SP when combo with [Magnificat](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Magnificat)).<br></br>
They can also be used for Eden Group Leveling Quests because of one of the best AoE skill aka [Metallic Fury](https://irowiki.org/wiki/Metallic_Fury) (260/55 patch onwards only, otherwise trash skill).</span>
</Collapse>

### 3rd Job (100-200)

As soon as you hit level 108, you should always go for party leveling as it's like 3-4x faster, no more Multi-Client, and leader will always have a Warp Portal too.
So please, just party leveling.
